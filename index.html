The goal of the game is to get your kick returner into the endzone without being tackled by the opposing players. In multiplayer mode the two of you switch back and forth until both of you have been tackled, and then the highest score is the winner.

Controls

  • Up: Move Forward
  • Down: Move Backwards
  • Left: Move Left
  • Right: Move Right
  • "a": Juke Left
  • "s": Juke Right
